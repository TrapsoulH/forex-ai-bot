What's included

Built for traders who want
more than a gut feeling.

Everything from signal generation to position management, in one place. No bloat, no unnecessary complexity.

Dual-gate signal engine

A signal only fires when both the technical analysis (RSI, EMA, MACD) and the ML model agree. That keeps false signals low and your attention on setups that actually matter.

Live trading dashboard

See your open positions, recent signals, and P&L at a glance. It refreshes every 30 seconds, so you're always looking at current data. Close a trade manually or let the bot handle it.

Risk controls

Set your stop-loss, take-profit, and max open trades once and leave them. Switch to paper trading mode any time to test your setup without touching real money.

Team access

Invite your team by email, set their role, and manage who has access — all from the admin panel. No manual account setup needed on their end.
